# GEMINI.md

## Core Principles
1. **Mandatory Consultation:** Consult the user and obtain explicit approval for all key architectural decisions, design patterns, or multi-component setups before implementation.
2. **Explicit Instruction:** Follow the user's direct instructions over assumptions. Do not assume that rules or conventions established for other agents (e.g., in `CLAUDE.md`) automatically apply to Gemini.
3. **Minimalist Approach:** Prioritize simple, single-purpose solutions (e.g., single-window tmux sessions) over complex setups unless otherwise requested.
4. **Context Independence:** Treat Gemini's operational logic as distinct and separate from other AI assistants in the environment unless the user explicitly directs otherwise.
