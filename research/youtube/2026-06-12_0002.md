## Parkev Tatevosian, CFA

In contrast, operating profit margins actually improved by 200 basis points to 67.3% as operating expenses remained flat, and the software segment—with 93% gross margins—saw revenue grow 9% to $7.2 billion. The key conclusion is that the sell-off was driven by misunderstood gross margin data, while the company’s core profitability, cash generation, and asset-light model remain exceptionally strong.
